Growing

You’ve heard people say “it grows like a weed,” and while that is true in some regards, your hemp plants will produce more CBD rich flower for you, with greater terpene intensity (smell), if you care for them attentively and provide them what they need, when they need it. For most home growers, that means plenty of sunlight, nutrient rich soil, and consistent watering. Knowing how much fertilizer and water to apply and when comes with experience, but I’ll help make it easy for you.
